Bank transactions & QuickBooks matching

Pull Purchases, Bills, and Bill Payments from QuickBooks for a date window, paste a bank export CSV (Date, Amount, Description), then run suggested matches by amount and date. Links you save here are an audit trail in this app only — they do not post to QuickBooks. Saved match links live in this app’s data store for traceability between bank lines and QBO documents. They are not QuickBooks journal entries or bank feeds. Use QuickBooks reconciliation for authoritative clearing. Use QuickBooks’ own reconciliation for final bank clearing.
